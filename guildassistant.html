    Description

    This discord bot is for guild's who are looking for less of a militarized discord bot such as Elder War General. Guild Assistant will keep track of guild resources,guild goals,guild roles,guild rules and much more. Guild Assistant is still currently in development and we are working hard to get a finished bot.

    Commands

    !guild resources 1: This will display the 1st page of the guild's resources
    !guild resources 2: This will display the 2nd page of the guild's resources
    !guild goals: This will display all of the guild's goals
    !guild rules: This will display all of the guild's rules
    !guild roles needed: This will display all of the roles needed in the guild
    !guild roles show: This will display the amount of members we have in each role
    !guild-roles apply: This will display all of the application requirements
