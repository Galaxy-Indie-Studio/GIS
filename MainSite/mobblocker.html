    Description

    Are you as tired of those phantoms as I am, While No more friends!!!!!
    With Mob Blocker, You can easily get rid of any mob known on minecraft (Up to July 8th,2021)!!!
    That is strictly what this plugin will be used for! It will only be updated when new mobs come out and when massive minecraft updates will affect the plugin, fix issues or update the ingame config!

    Commands
    =====================
    /mb block passive (MobName) true/false
    /mb block neutral (MobName) true/false
    /mb block hostile (MobName) true/false
    /mb block all true/false
    /mb reload config
