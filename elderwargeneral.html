    Elder War General Alpha 1.0v 12/07/2021 Invite







    Description:

    With this discord bot you will easily be able to keep track of your allies,enemies,guild resources, guild military resources, guild goals and much more. You won't have to remeber anymore stats or even track them down in a spread sheet, As Elder War General allows you to convinently do everything within discord!

    Commands

    !relations allies: This will display all of the guild's allies
    !relations enemies: This will display all of the guild's enemies
    !military enlist: This will display all of the requirements to enlist in the guild's military
    !military divisions: This will display all the divisions of the guild's military
    !military resources: This will display all available military resources
